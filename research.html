    Research Interests

    Music Generation

    Just as the first research paper is related to music generation, I am very interested in music generation. I like band sound and playing guitar, so I want to study about this. So, I want to decide whether to play solo with an acoustic guitar or electric guitar according to the atmosphere of the music and study a model that plays solo according to the progress of the music.

    Computer Vision

    I'm not as interested in music generation, but I'm also interested in computer vision. Since I didn't have many opportunities to hold music-related contests, I often worked on projects related to object detection. I didn't build the model myself, but I think it's attractive to have a sense of accomplishment when you recognize objects as you want through fine tuning. In the long run, I am thinking about developing a multimodal AI model that generates music based on the scenery while driving.
