  • A study on conditional music generation with GAN - PDF

    • This paper is about a model that adds genre conditions to the "Musegan" model to generate songs based on the conditions.

    This paper was written based on my first research experience as an undergraduate research student. I conducted this research through a university program called the 'Challenge Semester,' where I had to produce results within a given deadline. However, after implementing the model, the results were poor, and since I couldn't change the research topic, I eventually felt like I was doing it unwillingly. This made it a very disappointing study. Based on this experience, I plan to conduct thorough preliminary research before starting a project to prevent issues during the process. Additionally, I will plan and execute my projects more systematically.
